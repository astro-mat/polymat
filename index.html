Mat Warren - Musician

Mat Warren is an experimental guitarist, sound designer, recording engineer and producer known for his complex improvisational style. Using both traditional guitar techniques and also feeding it through software, utilising looping, generative techniques and complex signal-chains, He creates evocative sound-scapes that morph between ethereal organic pads to sheets of distorted noise.

Studio Moo Moo

Studio Moo Moo, an idyllic recording haven in rural north-west Ireland. A retreat for intimate songwriting and recording. Its one-room design seamlessly integrates live tracking with fostering a fluid creative process. Meticulous attention to lighting, decor, and top-notch acoustic design creates a cozy and inspiring atmosphere. Whether you're a solo artist or a group, Studio Moo Moo provides the perfect backdrop for your musical journey in the serene landscapes of rural Ireland.
